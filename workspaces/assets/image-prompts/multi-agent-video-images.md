# Visual Strategy: Multi-agent Video (The Vigilance)

This guide shows how to create a "Triangulated Intelligence" visual representing coordinated surveillance and active monitoring using high-end laser and obsidian themes.

---

### 1. Visual Intent: "The Coordinated Gaze"
**Concept**: A professional studio photograph of "The Watchtower Network." Four sleek, vertical obsidian prisms (The Agents) are arranged in a defensive circle on a dark charcoal platform. In the center of the circle, a small, highly detailed holographic 3D map (The Facility) is floating. Each prism projects a razor-sharp colored laser beam (some Cyan for observation, one Red for high-priority detection) into the central hologram, where they intersect.

**Target Vibe**: 
- Protective and alert
- High-security technology
- Precise and mathematical
- Moody and intense (Deep blacks, vibrant lasers)

---

### 2. Direct Gemini Prompt
> **Prompt**: "Professional industrial photography in a dark, minimalist studio. Four vertical, four-sided obsidian prisms with a high-polished finish are arranged in a circular formation on a dark platform. In the center of the formation, a small, highly detailed cyan holographic 3D grid of a building structure is floating. Each obsidian prism projects a single, razor-sharp laser beam—three are cyan, and one is a vibrant danger-red. The beams perfectly intersect at a point within the central hologram. Cinematic lighting, deep shadows, premium tech aesthetic. Sharp focus on the holographic intersection. No text, no artifacts. Shot on 85mm lens, f/1.8. 8k resolution, photorealistic stone and light textures."

---

### 3. Step-by-Step Gemini Interaction Guide

**Step 1: The Circle of Observation**
Submit the initial prompt. You are looking for a sense of "Unblinking Coordinated Security."

**Step 2: Refining the "Hologram"**
If the central structure looks like a blob, guide Gemini:
> *“Enhance the detail of the central hologram. It should look like a complex architectural blueprint made of glowing cyan light—thin lines, wireframes, and data points. It should be semi-translucent so the laser beams passing through it create realistic light refractions and glows.”*

**Step 3: Controlling the "Laser" intensity**
If the lasers look like thick light bars, ask for precision:
> *“The laser beams should be as thin as a strand of silk but incredibly intense. The point where the red laser hits the hologram should create a small, energetic 'alert' sparkle, showing the system has detected a specific, high-priority event.”*
