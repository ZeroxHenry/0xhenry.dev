# Visual Strategy: Client-Side AI (The Pocket Engine)

This guide shows how to create an "Internal Power" visual representing local AI inference and WebGPU using high-end glowing circuitry and glass themes.

---

### 1. Visual Intent: "Power From Within"
**Concept**: A professional studio photograph of "The Local Core." A sleek, modern smartphone or extremely thin laptop made of dark metal rests on a minimalist surface. The screen is turned off or completely black. The focus is *inside* the device. Visible through a section of frosted, transparent casing on the back/bottom, a tiny, incredibly complex, glowing cyan geometric engine (The SLM/WebGPU) is active. It casts a soft, vibrant light outwards, showing that the device is generating massive computational power entirely on its own, without any external cables or connections.

**Target Vibe**: 
- Self-contained and powerful
- Microscopic complexity
- High-end consumer electronics marketing aesthetic
- Contrast between "Dark Metal Exterior" and "Glowing Internal Engine"

---

### 2. Direct Gemini Prompt
> **Prompt**: "Professional product photography in a dark, minimalist studio. A sleek, ultra-thin laptop made of dark matte metal rests on a grey surface. A section of the laptop's casing is made of clear, frosted glass, revealing the internal components. Inside this clear section, a miniature, incredibly complex, glowing cyan geometric engine is active and brightly illuminated, representing local AI processing. There are no external cables or connections; all the power is self-contained. Cinematic lighting, deep shadows, premium tech aesthetic. Sharp focus on the glowing cyan internal engine. No text, no artifacts. Shot on 100mm macro lens, f/2.8. 8k resolution, photorealistic dark metal, glass, and light textures."

---

### 3. Step-by-Step Gemini Interaction Guide

**Step 1: The Internal Engine**
Submit the initial prompt. You are looking for a sense of "Untethered Computational Power."

**Step 2: Refining the "Engine" detail**
If the internal glowing engine looks just like a standard green motherboard, guide Gemini:
> *“The internal component must not look like a traditional circuit board. It should look like an 'AI Engine'—a dense, beautiful, 3D geometric structure made of pure cyan light and crystal, representing the neural network model running locally. It should be the absolute focal point of the image.”*

**Step 3: Controlling the "Self-Contained" aspect**
If there are glowing data streams flying into the laptop, ask for isolation:
> *“Ensure there are absolutely no external light beams, data streams, or wires connecting to the device. The entire point is that the AI power is 100% self-contained and local. The background should be a quiet, dark void to emphasize the glowing core inside the hardware.”*
