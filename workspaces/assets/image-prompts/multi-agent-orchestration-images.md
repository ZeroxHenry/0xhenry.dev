# Visual Strategy: Multi-Agent Orchestration (The Team)

This guide shows how to create a "Cooperative formation" visual representing a team of specialized agents using minimalist geometric themes.

---

### 1. Visual Intent: "Specialized Harmony"
**Concept**: A professional studio photograph of a "Formation of Intelligence." Centered in a dark charcoal space is a cluster of four glowing spheres. Each sphere has a slightly different size and a subtle, distinct color accent (e.g., one is pure white, one has a golden core, one a cyan core). They are connected by thin, precise light filaments, showing they are part of a single, coordinated system.

**Target Vibe**: 
- Organized and collaborative
- Precise and technical
- High-end museum aesthetic
- Sophisticated and balanced

---

### 2. Direct Gemini Prompt
> **Prompt**: "Professional 3D abstract photography in a dark, minimalist studio. A cluster of four glowing porcelain-like spheres of varying sizes is suspended in a dark charcoal space. Each sphere emits a soft internal glow—one pure white, one cyan-tinted, and one gold-tinted. Elegant, thin white light filaments connect each sphere to the others, forming a cohesive geometric structure. High-end lighting, deep bokeh, sharp focus on the central larger sphere. Clean composition, no text, no artifacts, premium tech aesthetic. Shot on 85mm lens, f/2.0. 8k resolution, photorealistic porcelain textures."

---

### 3. Step-by-Step Gemini Interaction Guide

**Step 1: The Collective Capture**
Submit the initial prompt. You are looking for a sense of "A Team working as One."

**Step 2: Refining the "Specialization"**
If the spheres look identical, guide Gemini:
> *“Make the differences between the spheres more intentional. The 'Lead' sphere should be slightly larger and have a brighter, pure white glow. The 'Specialist' spheres should be smaller and have different internal color cores. Ensure the porcelain texture has a high-quality matte finish.”*

**Step 3: Controlling the Connections**
If the filaments are too busy, ask for cleanliness:
> *“Simplify the connections so only the most vital paths are shown. The white filaments should be as thin as a single strand of hair but perfectly sharp and glowing. The background should be a deep, clean charcoal with no distraction, making the 'Team' the only subject.”*
