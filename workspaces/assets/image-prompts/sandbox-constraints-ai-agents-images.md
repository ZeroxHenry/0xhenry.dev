# Visual Strategy: Sandbox Constraints (The Glass Prison)

This guide shows how to create a "Contained Power" visual representing sandboxing and security constraints for AI agents using high-end glass and containment themes.

---

### 1. Visual Intent: "Dangerous Beauty Contained"
**Concept**: A professional studio photograph of "The Containment Chamber." Inside a massive, dark architectural space, a small, perfectly square, thick glass room is illuminated. Inside the glass room, a chaotic, intense, and hyper-powerful explosion of glowing cyan and magenta energy (The Agent executing code) is occurring. However, the glass is holding perfectly. Outside the room, the environment is calm, dark, and completely undisturbed, visually proving that whatever happens inside the sandbox cannot affect the outside world.

**Target Vibe**: 
- Absolute security
- Controlled chaos
- High-end architectural and physics-based aesthetic
- Contrast between "Internal Chaos" and "External Calm"

---

### 2. Direct Gemini Prompt
> **Prompt**: "Professional conceptual photography in a vast, dark, minimalist studio. In the center sits a single, perfectly geometric, thick glass cube representing a high-security sandbox. Inside the cube, a violent and beautiful storm of glowing cyan and magenta data particles and energy is swirling rapidly. Despite the intensity inside, the surfaces of the glass cube remain perfectly smooth and intact, not even vibrating. Outside the cube, the environment is silent, calm, and captured in deep, elegant shadows. Cinematic lighting, premium tech aesthetic. Sharp focus on the refractive edges of the glass cube. No text, no artifacts. Shot on 85mm lens, f/1.8. 8k resolution, photorealistic glass, light energy, and atmospheric smoke textures."

---

### 3. Step-by-Step Gemini Interaction Guide

**Step 1: The Chamber**
Submit the initial prompt. You are looking for a sense of "Boundless Power in a Bound Box."

**Step 2: Refining the "Energy"**
If the energy looks like messy lines or smoke, guide Gemini:
> *“The energy inside should look like high-velocity digital fragments—bits of code, glowing data triangles, and sharp streaks of light. It should look like it's trying to get out but is effortlessly reflected back by the glass walls. This symbolizes that even a compromised or rogue AI cannot break the container.”*

**Step 3: Controlling the "Glass"**
If the cube looks like a plastic box, ask for weight:
> *“The glass must look extremely thick and high-end, with beautiful refractive edges where the internal light hits the corners. It should feel like a solid, unshakeable physical constraint. The environment outside should be almost pitch black to make the containment chamber the sole, luminous hero of the image.”*
