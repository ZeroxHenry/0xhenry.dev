# Visual Strategy: Code RAG (The Logic Circuit)

This guide shows how to create a "Structural Intelligence" visual that represents code architecture using premium electronics photography.

---

### 1. Visual Intent: "The Blueprint of Logic"
**Concept**: A macro photograph of a high-end, matte black PCB. Instead of raw components, the focus is on the golden traces which are glowing with a warm, internal light. The traces should look like a complex urban map or a 3D architectural blueprint, symbolizing the "Structural Awareness" of Code RAG.

**Target Vibe**: 
- High-end electronics photography
- Golden and Charcoal color palette
- Technical and precise
- Abstract but recognizable as a circuit

---

### 2. Direct Gemini Prompt
> **Prompt**: "Macro extreme close-up photography of a pristine matte black circuit board. Elegant golden traces sprawl across the surface like a microscopic 3D glowing city map. The traces have a soft, golden internal amber glow. Sharp focus on a specific junction point in the center, with a deep cinematic bokeh and shallow depth of field in the background. Professional studio lighting, realistic metallic textures, no artifacts, no text. Clean, minimalist, premium tech aesthetic. Shot on 100mm macro lens, f/2.8. 8k resolution, photorealistic."

---

### 3. Step-by-Step Gemini Interaction Guide

**Step 1: The Golden Root**
Submit the initial prompt. You are looking for that dark, premium "Apple-style" interior look.

**Step 2: Refining the Glow**
If the golden traces look like flat lines, ask Gemini:
> *“Make the golden traces look like they are 3D etched into the black surface. Increase the internal amber glow so they look like they are carrying data/power. The light should catch the edges of the traces realistically.”*

**Step 3: Depth and Detail**
If the image is too flat, guide it:
> *“Increase the blur of the background components to emphasize the scale and complexity of the circuitry. Ensure the matte black texture of the board has a subtle grain and realistic light falloff.”*
