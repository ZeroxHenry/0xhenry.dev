# Visual Strategy: Speculative Decoding (The Prevision)

This guide shows how to create a "Predictive Speed" visual representing large-small model synergy using high-end kinetic and glass themes.

---

### 1. Visual Intent: "Anticipated Motion"
**Concept**: A professional studio photograph of "The Future Path." A large, heavy matte charcoal sphere (The Target Model) is centered. Behind it, a ghostly, perfectly translucent glowing white sphere (The Draft Model) is "ghosting" forward—blurred as it moves fast to predict the next position. A trail of small glowing data points (Tokens) leads ahead of them both.

**Target Vibe**: 
- Dynamic and fast
- Sophisticated kinetic art
- Contrast between "Mass" and "Speed"
- Premium tech aesthetic

---

### 2. Direct Gemini Prompt
> **Prompt**: "Professional kinetic photography in a dark, minimalist studio. A large, heavy matte charcoal stone sphere sits in the center of a dark platform. Behind it, a perfectly translucent glass sphere is captured in sharp motion blur as it 'leaps' ahead. A trail of tiny, razor-sharp glowing white sparks leads into the far distance, showing a predicted path. Cinematic side lighting, deep shadows, high-end tech aesthetic. Sharp focus on the heavy stone sphere, with artistic motion blur on the glass sphere. No text, no artifacts. Shot on 100mm macro lens, f/2.0. 8k resolution, photorealistic stone and glass textures."

---

### 3. Step-by-Step Gemini Interaction Guide

**Step 1: The Initial Leap**
Submit the initial prompt. You are looking for a sense of "A small thing pulling a big thing along."

**Step 2: Refining the "Ghosting"**
If the glass sphere looks too solid, guide Gemini:
> *“Increase the transparency of the moving sphere. It should look like a ghostly after-image or a holographic projection that is calculating the path ahead of the heavy stone sphere. The motion blur should be clean and directional, pointing forward.”*

**Step 3: Controlling the Path**
If the path of sparks looks like a mess, ask for order:
> *“The trail of glowing sparks should look like a precise, mathematical line—a sequence of data points. Each spark represents a 'correct' prediction. Ensure the lighting on the stone sphere highlights its rough texture, contrasting with the ethereal sparks.”*
