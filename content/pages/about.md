## About TechPulse Engineering

TechPulse Engineering is a technology blog dedicated to practical engineering knowledge. We cover robotics, embedded systems, programming, 3D printing, IoT, and emerging technologies.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic engineering concepts and real-world applications. Every article is crafted to provide actionable insights that engineers and developers can apply immediately.

### What We Cover

- **Robotics & Automation** — From servo control to complete robot builds
- **Embedded Systems** — Microcontroller programming, sensor integration, real-time systems
- **Programming** — Python for engineers, algorithm design, data processing
- **Electronics** — Circuit design, PCB prototyping, signal processing
- **3D Printing** — Design for manufacturing, material selection, printer optimization
- **AI & Machine Learning** — Practical ML applications in engineering
