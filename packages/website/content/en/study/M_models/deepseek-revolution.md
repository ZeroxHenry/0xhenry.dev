One of the hottest topics in the AI community lately is undoubtedly **DeepSeek.** They are posing the question, "Why should we use expensive APIs?" with their incredible price-performance ratio.

We take a look at the shift in the LLM landscape that DeepSeek has brought about.

---

### 1. Extreme Price-Performance: DeepSeek-V2.5

DeepSeek's latest models often show GPT-4o-level intelligence while being priced at less than 1/10th the cost.
- **Meaning for Agent Developers**: In an agent service that requires hundreds of tool calls and thought processes, reducing API costs directly correlates with business profitability.

---

### 2. Coding-Specialized Model: DeepSeek-Coder

DeepSeek first gained attention through its coding prowess.
- **Performance**: It consistently ranks #1 among open-source coding models in numerous benchmarks. It is particularly excellent at understanding the syntax of modern web frameworks like React and Next.js.

---

### 3. Transparent Technical Disclosure

They disclose how they trained their models and built their datasets in relatively high detail via technical reports. This provides important learning material for late-comers and independent developers.

---

### Henry's Take: "From Rebels to Leading Roles"

DeepSeek is no longer just a 'cheap alternative.' In some areas (particularly coding), it has become the 'top priority' to consider. If your agent pipeline involves repetitive coding tasks or data extraction, I highly recommend DeepSeek.
