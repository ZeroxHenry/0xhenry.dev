At the end of 2024, the world was shocked when Meta announced **Llama-3.1 405B.** "A freely available model is on par with the paid GPT-4o?"

I’ve summarized the tectonic shift Llama-3.1 caused in the AI agent ecosystem and why we must pay attention to this model.

---

### 1. 405B: The Zenith of Open Source Intelligence

Previously, open-source models were merely 'small and light but less intelligent' alternatives. The 405B model changed that. It stands shoulder-to-shoulder with closed models in complex reasoning, math, and multilingual processing. Companies can now build the **'smartest brain'** on their internal servers without sending data outside.

---

### 2. The Magic of Distillation

The greatest value of the 405B model is that it can **teach smaller models (Distillation).** Legally transferring the knowledge of 405B to 8B or 70B models allows high-performance reasoning on low-spec devices—a true 'inheritance of knowledge.'

---

### 3. More Powerful Tool Calling and Context

Llama-3.1 was born for agent development. Its generous 128K context window and ability to precisely call external APIs have been dramatically improved. Now, you can perfectly implement agents that search the web and modify DBs using only Llama.

---

### Henry's Take: "The Privatization of Intelligence is Over"

The era where intelligence was the exclusive possession of a few mega-corporations is over. Llama-3.1 has placed a powerful weapon in the hands of every developer. The moment you put Llama on your local server, you gain sovereignty over your own intelligence.
