Amidst the technical hegemony of Silicon Valley, there’s a company guarding European pride: France's **Mistral AI.** Their **Mistral Large 2** doesn't captivate developers just because it's a 'European model,' but because of its sheer performance.

Let's dive into the allure of this model specifically specialized in multilingual processing.

---

### 1. Flawless Support for 80+ Languages

Many models excel in English but drop significantly in performance for languages like Korean, French, or German.
- **Feature**: Mistral Large 2 was designed with multilinguality in mind from the ground up. It’s excellent at grasping the nuances of Korean and is considered one of the most suitable models for global agent services targeting international markets.

---

### 2. The Zenith of Efficiency: The Power of 123B

Massive models like Llama-3.1 405B have infrastructure costs that are hard to bear. However, Mistral Large 2 delivers performance nearing that of 405B with a relatively reasonable size of **123B.**
- **Benefit**: It is overwhelming in terms of price-performance (Efficiency). This means you can run smarter agents with fewer resources.

---

### 3. Coding, Math, and Sophisticated Tool Calling

Mistral has traditionally shown strength in coding ability. The Large 2 model also shows top-tier performance in major languages like JavaScript and Python, with very high reliability in **Function Calling,** a core agent feature.

---

### Henry's Take: "The Quiet Giant of Global Business"

If your service targets the global market, Mistral Large 2 might be a smarter companion than an English-centric American model. European refined intelligence will add depth to your agents.
